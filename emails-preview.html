Sending via Resend

  • Add & verify your sender domain in Resend (DNS: SPF, DKIM, DMARC). Use a sender like rsvp@chateau-prive.com.
  • Each emails/0X-*.html file is self-contained — open, replace {{tokens}}, send.
  • From the API: POST /emails with html body. Use Resend's React/JSX templating or simple string replace.
  • For the auto-reply (01), wire it to your Formspree webhook or send it directly from your own backend on form submit.
  • Approval (02) and Decline (03) are sent manually — paste the HTML into Resend's UI and replace tokens, or build a small admin form.
  • Reminder (04) and Day-of (05) — schedule via Resend's scheduling, or a cron job on your server.
  • All templates: 600px max, table-based, inline-styled, Google Fonts with Georgia fallback. Tested layouts work in Gmail, Apple Mail, Outlook (modern), iOS, Android.
